      v1.0 - June 3, 2016: Break Scheduling, Whitelisting, Languages & more!

      Drumroll please.. Round 2 of features have arrived! The most requested functionality is now available. What can you do?

          1. Schedule your break times! Disable GFW when you're not working so there aren't any nasty surprises. Add the times you need a break on the options page.
          2. Have exceptions that you want to allow? Add them to the whitelist. Find it in your advanced features.
          3. A lot of you reported that you couldn't go back to the blocked page after pausing. That's fixed now! Hit back to visit the last page GFW blocked (if you're paused or on break).
          4. French and Russian translations are now up. If your browser uses those languages, you'll automatically see translated messages!

      Special thanks are in order! Thank you Chris Lewis for helping with the Whitelist feature. Check him out at @cmlewis89.

      Also, thank you to Vincent Dassens for translating GFW to French and to Evgenia Fadzeyeva for translating to Russian. Find Vincent's Twitter at @VincentDassens.

      v0.5 - March 21, 2016: Introducing Advanced Settings

      You asked for features. I listened to some of you. Introducing advanced features. Scroll to the bottom of the options and hit the big button. Can't miss it. What can you do?

          1. You can now force refresh pages when your break is up. No more exploiting that 'loophole.' Enable it in advanced settings.
          2. For the hardcore, pausing can be disabled. Only for the super productive among us. Enable it in advance settings.

      More features coming soon. On the deck are whitelists and scheduled break times.
